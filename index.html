The Interactive System for Assisting the Visually Impaired is a comprehensive and intuitive solution that employs advanced technology to provide assistance, accessibility, and independence to individuals with visual impairments. By integrating object detection, speech synthesis, eye gaze tracking, red ball detection, time announcement, facial recognition, and emotion detection, this system offers a range of functionalities tailored to the unique needs of visually impaired users. We hope this system enhances the quality of life and empowers individuals with visual impairments to navigate their environment with greater ease.
